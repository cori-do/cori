Motion principles

Alive, not noisy.

  1. 01

    Progress leaves a trace.

    Every completed orbit becomes a stable fact. The animation never replaces status, elapsed time, or cost.

  2. 02

    Pauses open; they do not spin.

    When Cori needs a person, the system physically makes room for them and motion nearly stops.

  3. 03

    Color is energy before it is decoration.

    Violet is the core. Cyan and pink travel through it. Green, amber, and red remain semantic.

  4. 04

    The mark always comes home.

    Idle and success resolve to the exact orbit-and-core silhouette from the supplied Cloud exploration.
